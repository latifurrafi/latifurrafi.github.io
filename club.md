### Overview

As Organizing Secretary of the **Daffodil AI Club**, I design and coordinate activities that connect students with practical AI engineering, research culture, and community building. Below is a selection of seminars, contests, hackathons, and courses I have organized or led.

---

### Seminars & Webinars

- **Inauguration Ceremony – Daffodil AI Club**  
  Helped plan and run the formal launch event of the club, introducing its mission, leadership structure, and initial roadmap to students and faculty.

- **Digital Twin Seminar: Transforming the Future of Intelligent Systems**  
  Organized and chaired a technical seminar introducing digital twin concepts and their applications in intelligent, data-driven systems.

- **Online Webinar on AI Engineering**  
  Coordinated and moderated an online webinar highlighting modern AI engineering practices, tooling, and industry pathways for students.

- **Opportunity as CSE Students in Foreign Countries**  
  Led an information session outlining study, scholarship, and career opportunities for CSE students in international contexts.

---

### Contests & Hackathons

- **Logo Design Contest – Daffodil AI Club**  
  Conceived and managed a club-wide logo competition, defining design guidelines, evaluation criteria, and a transparent judging process to establish a consistent visual identity.

- **ModalX-AI Contest**  
  Designed and organized an AI-focused contest where participants developed and deployed intelligent models, emphasizing practical problem solving, reproducible experimentation, and responsible AI practices.

### Courses

- **Course Coordinator, “AI Engineering MasterClass 2026”**  
  Designed the course module and syllabus, coordinated instruction between faculty and industry mentors, and oversaw logistics and continuous support for approximately 35 enrolled students, ensuring sustained engagement with modern AI engineering tools and workflows.
