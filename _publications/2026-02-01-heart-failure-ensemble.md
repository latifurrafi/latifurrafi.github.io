This study proposes a comparative ensemble machine-learning framework for clinical decision support in heart-failure prediction. Random Forest, LightGBM, and XGBoost are evaluated across clinical feature sets, with XGBoost achieving the highest accuracy while maintaining balanced sensitivity and interpretability — supporting reliable, explainable clinical decision-making.

*Accepted at the 2026 International Conference on Engineering and Frontier Technologies (ICEFronT), IEEE.*
