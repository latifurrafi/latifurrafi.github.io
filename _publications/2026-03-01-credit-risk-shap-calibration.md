This work develops a unified credit-risk assessment framework on the German Credit dataset that jointly optimizes discrimination, calibration, and explainability. The pipeline combines gradient-boosted modeling with 5-fold cross-validation (CV &lt; 12%), reliability-diagram calibration analysis (Brier Score), and SHAP-based feature attribution with feature-stability validation. Among the compared models, LightGBM achieved a 0.756 ROC-AUC with superior calibration.

*Accepted at the 2026 International Conference on Engineering and Frontier Technologies (ICEFronT), IEEE.*
