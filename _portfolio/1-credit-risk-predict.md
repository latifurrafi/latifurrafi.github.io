Implementation behind the accepted **ICEFronT 2026 (IEEE)** publication — a unified credit-risk framework on the German Credit dataset integrating discrimination, calibration, and explainability.

- Built the full pipeline: preprocessing, gradient-boosted modeling (LightGBM, ROC-AUC 0.756), 5-fold stability validation (CV &lt; 12%), bias/fairness checks, and SHAP-based calibration and reliability plots.

**Tools:** Python, LightGBM, XGBoost, Scikit-learn, SHAP
